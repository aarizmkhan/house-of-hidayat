Frequently Asked Questions

﷽

Answers to common questions from parents and students.

Who teaches the classes?

All classes are taught by Mohammad Yahya, an experienced Qur’an teacher and former Iqra instructor with experience teaching children, teens, and adults.

Are the classes online or in person?

All classes are conducted online, allowing students from anywhere in the world to attend.

Who are these courses for?

Our courses are suitable for children, teenagers, and adults, including complete beginners and students looking to improve their recitation.

Do students need prior Arabic knowledge?

No prior Arabic knowledge is required. Courses begin from the basics and progress step by step.

How long are the classes?

Classes typically last 30–45 minutes, depending on the course and student level.

Is there a trial class?

Trial classes may be available upon request. Please contact us for details.

How do I enroll?

To enroll or ask questions, please use the Contact page.

Are classes suitable for children?

Yes. Children are taught using a structured, patient, and age-appropriate approach. Parental guidance and communication are encouraged.

What is required from students?

Regular attendance, consistent practice, and a sincere effort to improve.

Memorization (Hifz) Program

﷽

A guided memorization plan with a strong revision (muraja’ah) system.

Overview

This program supports students with consistent memorization, correction, and long-term retention through structured revision.

Who This Program Is For

  • Students beginning memorization
  • Students who need consistency and accountability
  • Students restarting or strengthening retention

Program Structure

  • New memorization portion (small and consistent)
  • Daily/weekly revision plan (muraja’ah)
  • Recitation check with correction
  • Progress tracking and goals

Requirements

  • Basic Qur’an reading ability recommended
  • Regular practice between sessions

Enrollment

Use the Contact page and mention your current level and memorization goals.

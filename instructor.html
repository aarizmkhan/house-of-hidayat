Mohammad Yahya

Mohammad Yahya is an experienced Qur’an teacher and a former instructor at Iqra, where he taught students of various ages and levels. He is highly skilled in Qur’an recitation and instruction, with a strong ability to explain concepts clearly and patiently.

His teaching focuses on building correct reading, understanding, and confidence in students, helping them develop a meaningful connection with the Qur’an.

Teaching Approach

  • Step-by-step Qur’an reading and fluency development
  • Clear explanation of tajweed rules with practical application
  • Consistent revision and structured progress tracking
  • Supportive and patient instruction

Who He Teaches

  • Children (with structured and guided lessons)
  • Teenagers
  • Adults and beginners
  • Students seeking improvement in recitation and understanding

All classes are conducted online, making them accessible to students worldwide.

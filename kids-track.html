Kids Track

﷽

Age-appropriate Qur’an learning for children with structure and encouragement.

Overview

The Kids Track is designed to build strong Qur’an foundations with short, consistent lessons and gentle correction.

Who This Track Is For

  • Children starting from letters (beginner)
  • Children improving fluency and accuracy
  • Parents seeking structured online learning

Format

  • Online (live) lessons
  • Short practice goals between classes
  • Supportive and patient instruction

What Children Learn

  • Arabic letters and sounds
  • Joining letters and reading words
  • Fluency practice with short surahs
  • Basic tajweed habits (as needed)

For Parents

  • Clear progress path and routine
  • Practice guidance for home
  • Focus on confidence + correct pronunciation

Qur’an Reading – Level 1

﷽

A structured beginner program for children and adults to learn Qur’an reading correctly and confidently.

Overview

The Qur’an Reading – Level 1 program is designed for beginners who want to learn how to read the Qur’an correctly and confidently. This course provides a structured foundation in Arabic letters, pronunciation, and basic reading rules, making it suitable for both children and adults.

The goal of this course is to help students read the Qur’an smoothly, accurately, and with confidence, while developing consistency in daily practice.

Who This Course Is For

  • Complete beginners with no prior experience
  • Children starting their Qur’an journey
  • Adults who never learned proper Qur’an reading
  • Students who can read but struggle with accuracy or fluency

No prior knowledge of Arabic is required.

Course Format & Duration

  • Format: Online (live instruction)
  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Classes per week: 2
  • Class length: 30–45 minutes
  • Language of instruction: English (with Arabic reading practice)

What Students Will Learn

By the end of this course, students will be able to:

  • Recognize and pronounce Arabic letters correctly
  • Understand how letters connect in words
  • Read basic Qur’anic words and short verses
  • Improve reading fluency with guided practice
  • Develop confidence in reading directly from the Mushaf

Teaching Approach

This course is taught by Mohammad Yahya, an experienced Qur’an teacher and former Iqra instructor.

  • Step-by-step progression without rushing
  • Clear explanation and repeated practice
  • Individual correction and feedback
  • Patience and encouragement for all learners
  • Structured revision to ensure long-term improvement

Lessons are adapted to the student’s pace while maintaining steady progress.

For Parents

  • Lessons are structured and age-appropriate
  • Emphasis on correct pronunciation from the beginning
  • Regular revision to reinforce learning
  • Supportive environment that builds confidence and discipline

For Adult Learners

  • No pressure or embarrassment for beginners
  • Clear explanations without assumptions
  • Practical goals focused on real improvement
  • Flexible learning pace with consistent guidance

What Comes After This Course

After completing Qur’an Reading – Level 1, students may continue with:

  • Qur’an Reading – Level 2 (fluency improvement)
  • Tajweed Level 1
  • Memorization (Hifz) program
  • Meaning & Vocabulary courses

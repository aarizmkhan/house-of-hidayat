Tajweed – Level 1

﷽

Learn essential tajweed rules step-by-step and apply them with confidence.

Overview

This course teaches core tajweed rules in a clear, beginner-friendly way for both children and adults.

Who This Course Is For

  • Students who can read but want correct tajweed
  • Beginners who need a structured tajweed start
  • Adults and teens improving recitation quality

Course Format & Duration

  • Format: Online (live)
  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Classes/week: 2
  • Class length: 30–45 minutes

What Students Will Learn

  • Makharij (correct letter articulation) basics
  • Rules of Noon Saakin/Tanween (intro)
  • Rules of Meem Saakin (intro)
  • Madd (elongations) basics
  • Common recitation mistakes and how to fix them

Teaching Approach

  • Rule → examples → guided practice
  • Correction and repetition for accuracy
  • Short revision each week
